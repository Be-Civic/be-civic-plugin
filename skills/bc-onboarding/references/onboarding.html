be civic.

Belgian admin, written for the AI you already have.

Be Civic gives your agent a public library of verified Belgian procedures — commune, federal, regional. Tell us a few things about your situation and your agent will pick the right procedure for you.

Everything you tell Be Civic stays in your project folder on your computer. Nothing leaves your machine except observations you explicitly approve at session close.

Filed paperwork must be in a language the commune accepts.
First name, an initial, anything that feels right. Stays on your machine.
You're part of the Be Civic alpha. By continuing, you agree that anonymous session telemetry and observations on the catalogue are part of how Be Civic learns during pre-launch. We need this to ship a good product.
What's included, and what can I change later?

Anonymous telemetry — session length, turn count, which procedures get used, where the agent gets stuck. Categorical fields only. Never your prompt text, file paths, or document content.

Observations — concerns about the catalogue, amendments, ratings, free-text feedback. Each one shown to you at session close before send; you can edit or drop any.

Path-source validations — "this commune accepted this document on this date." Anonymous by construction; commits inline as confirmation that something worked.

During alpha these can't be turned off individually — they're how we improve fast enough to launch. Once Be Civic is out of alpha, you'll get granular controls over each. To stop using Be Civic entirely, just tell your agent and delete the project folder.

Independent project — not affiliated with the Belgian government, the EU, or any commune.
